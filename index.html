About Tibia

Tibia is a free massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) created by CipSoft in 1997.

For over two decades, Tibia has been captivating players with its unique blend of classic 2D graphics, challenging gameplay, and a thriving community. Set in a medieval fantasy world, players embark on epic adventures, battle fearsome creatures, complete intricate quests, and forge alliances with fellow adventurers.

Getting Started

Your journey begins here

Visit the official Tibia website and click on "Create Account". Fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start your adventure!

Knights are great for beginners due to high health. Paladins offer versatility. Sorcerers and Druids are powerful but require skill. Start with what appeals to your playstyle!

Complete quests, hunt creatures appropriate to your level, and join a team for better experience gains. Always keep your skills trained!

Yes! Tibia is free to play. Premium accounts offer additional content, faster skill training, and access to exclusive areas, but the core game is completely free.

Reach level 20, find a guild that matches your playstyle, and apply. Many guilds recruit actively in-game or on forums. Being part of a guild enhances your Tibia experience!
